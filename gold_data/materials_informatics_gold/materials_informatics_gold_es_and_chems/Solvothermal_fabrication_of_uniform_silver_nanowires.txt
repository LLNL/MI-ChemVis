Briefly, 0.087 g PVP was dissolved in 10 mL EG solution of CuCl (20 μM), and then 10 mL EG solution of AgNO3 (0.05 M) was quickly added into the above solution. The mixed solution was stirred for about 1 min and put into a 25 mL Teflon-lined autoclave. This autoclave was then sealed and heated at 160 °C for 2.5 h. Afterwards, the solution was cooled to room temperature.
