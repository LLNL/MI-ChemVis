An aqueous solution (0.075 ml) of 200 mM Na2PdCl4 was mixed with 20 ml H2O in a beaker, followed by the addition of 6 mg trisodium citrate. After 5 min, a 3 ml aqueous solution including 6 mg trisodium citrate and 3 mg KBH4 was injected into the beaker at a low flow rate. The reaction lasted for 30 min with constant stirring.
